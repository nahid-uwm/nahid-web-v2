// 01 — Profile

About

I'm fascinated by design and technology that make life easier and more enjoyable — from the bumpy yellow dots on sidewalks to 3D glasses. The little things that quietly make life more accessible always make me ask: how do we build things that feel more humane?

With virtual reality and AI shaping the future, I want to direct these tools toward human needs — wellness, safety, and perception.

Current focus: VR locomotion and simulation studies for pedestrian safety, including driver blind-spot evaluation.
